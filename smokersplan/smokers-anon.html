Smokers Anonymous

Ian Barnie

WDD 130-section 17

Overview

Purpose

The idea of Smokers Anonymous is to bring people of any background, whether it be religious, ethnic, race or any other differences in human definition and work out why these persons have trouble quitting or staying away from smoking specifically cigarettes. Many people find that smoking is addictive but many people that do smoke do not have people to talk to about why it is that they smoke or how to recover from it. Now, while it may not be like drinking or other drug abuse substances, as it doesn't impair judgement, it has been found in many different studies to have the same addictive properties as that of cocaine which means that it needs to be treated with the same level of care as any other drugs should be and are while sadly smoking cigarettes has not been.

Audience

Now then, as some of the details of smoking and the idea behind why smoking needs to be treated with care have been given in the purpose behind this site it is now imperative to speak about why it can effect your day to day life. You will often find yourself pausing a show to smoke, which as a single person may not be that detrimental, but as a member of a family can cause social drifting between you and your spouse and/or children. Without a cigarette you will start to feel angry much more easily finding interation to be incredibly difficult while also causing anxiety and depression as well. So I want to start by asking this, do you smoke because it is a good way to avoid these feelings? Many people try to smoke to avoid the one thing that will be just as hard if not harder to deal with once quitting. It doesn't really give any real solution but a temperary one, and also decreases your desire to eat sometimes eating only once a day and drinking water causing immense dehydration. I want to ask you all today to come together on this site and even in person to talk about why it is that you smoke, what made you start and what you feel could help you to quit as well as how to combat the reasons you do smoke and find much more healthy ways to deal with these reasons for doing so in the first place.
